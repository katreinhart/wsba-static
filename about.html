About WSBA

The Washington State Bicycle Association is the governing body for competitive cycling in the state of Washington, as well as Oregon and the Northern Idaho region.

As a USA Cycling local association, WSBA sanctions competitve events in road, track, cyclocross, and mountain biking. Additionally it supports all types of competitive cycling, sponsors programs for junior racers, and is home to a thriving community of racers of all ages.
